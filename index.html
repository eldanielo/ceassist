CE Assistant

How to Use

  1. Click the Record from Tab button above.
  2. In the pop-up, select the browser tab with your Google Meet call. Make sure to enable tab audio sharing.
  3. Enable Gemini transcription in Google Meet. This informs the customer that the call is being transcribed.
  4. Switch back to this CE Assistant tab to see the live analysis.
